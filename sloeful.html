Learning Guide

Play the audio and see how much you can understand - the main ideas, and familiar words and phrases.

Read through the text slowly. When a word is unfamiliar, guess the meaning first, before clicking the word to check the English.

Take the comprehension quiz and test your understanding.

Use the flashcards to recall useful key words - say the answer out loud before you flip.
